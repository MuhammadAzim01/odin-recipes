Fry an egg

Descriptioon

Whisk eggs in a bowl with salt and pepper, melt butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat, add eggs to the pan, stir gently until cooked, and serve.


Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1-2 teaspoons butter or oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a small non-stick frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Add 1-2 teaspoons of butter or oil to the pan.
  3. Crack the egg into the pan carefully and gently. Be careful not to break the yolk.
  4. Let the egg cook for 2-3 minutes, or until the whites are set and the edges are slightly browned and crispy.
  5. Use a spatula to carefully flip the egg over, and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes until the yolk is cooked to your desired level of doneness.
  6. Remove the egg from the pan and place it on a plate.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
